History of Chai Jaai

Ancient Times
Tea arrives in Kashmir via the Silk Road from China and Central Asia, becoming part of local culture.
17th–18th Century
Mughal influence brings new tea traditions; Kahwa and Noon Chai become Kashmiri staples.
1800s
Samovar (Russian kettle) and bone china cups become popular for serving tea in Kashmir.
2016
Chai Jaai is founded in Srinagar by Muneer Kabir, blending Kashmiri and English tea room culture.
2017–2024
Chai Jaai becomes a cultural icon, known for its ambiance, artisan teaware, and 60+ tea varieties.
